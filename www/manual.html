Story

In times of long jurneys ther were the moments of curage and curiousity. This story begins simple. With one ship and a bunch of other pirates like ayou are in the middle of an ocean. You know that the treasure is near. You feel it in Your wooden leg every morning stronger. And today is the day.

You found the island!

But soon You'll find out You were not the first..

Game mechanics

It's simple turn-based strategy game. We have two different races: Pirates and Skeletons. Pirates goal is to conquer the island, find all the treasures and whipe out skeletons. The skeletons goal is oposite.
