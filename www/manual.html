Game mechanics

It's simple turn-based strategy game. We have two different teams: Pirates vs. Skeletons. First turn is the pirates. You start with a ship. First things to do is to sail to the land. There you'll be able to produce some units and start to search for the legendary treasure. When you cant take any move press [END TURN] to end turn. Give device to Your friend.

Next are skeletons. Press start when you're ready. You have the first cementary. Buy some unit.

Moving units

When unit is selected eery square around it is highlited. There are feve options that may apear:

  • white circle - move here
  • cross - group this units
  • red circle - attack here
  • $ - here will land units that you produce

Each action takes one turn and marks unit as ready for next turn.

Producing units

Some units have ability to producing. When selected you'll see icons of units to buy. You can see only those units that you can afford at that time.

Unit prices

Attacking

When units are in figth they acualy play a game of dices. Evey unit represents a one dice. Each unit rolls a dice and the higher wins. Units in squad attacks in pairs for each unit in squad individualy.

Pirates

Only one thing in their minds: treasure. It's chest full of gold. Land unitc can open chests. Each open chest gives pirates +$20.

  • Ship It's main headquater. It can sail. Wenever ship is near land it can produce land units. Every turn pirates gains $ from each ship sailing.

  • Pirate Juts a pirate. Stoll very agressive. Attacks nerby units. Can group with another pirates to forming a squad. Max to a 6 unit in it.

  • Porate with gun This pirate can shoot at distance of 3 blocks. After every shoot he must reload the gun with results in a 1 turn waiting. Those pirates can forming a squad too.

  • Lumberjack He moves only one block at a time. Can chop trees and it's the only unit that can destroy cementary. His axe chopes infinite number of trees but it can by used only once on a cementary. After that unit becomes a normal pirate unit. Lumberjack works alone. It can not form a squad.

Skeletons

They on the island for a long time. They were born from lost souls of difitted mens. They only desire is to keep the treasure out of pirate's hands.

Land units can close ounce opened chest. For every closed chest skeletons gains +$50.

  • Kraken “Kraken are legendary sea monsters of giant proportions said to dwell off the coasts of Norway and Greenland.” - Wikipedia Well.. It seems that they are here too. This unit can destroy any ship with easy.

  • Cementary It's main base for skeletons. Here you can produce other units. Cementary can be destroyed by lumberjack. Every turn each cementary gives skeletons $.

  • Skeleton They identical to pirates. Can form a squad

  • Dust It's evil unit. Dust looks like huge ball of evilness sorounded by flames and floating in the air. Have a big range of affack and put down instantly any land oponent. But there's a hack, it dies right after attack.
