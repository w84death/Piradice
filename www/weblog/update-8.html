The map

Now the game looks like it was played on a real map laying on the table. It was my purpose from the beginning to simulate table-top game. And for that feeling I turned off fog of war feature. It was too much WarCraft-ish :)

Gameplay

  • each turn player is given 5 gold and 1 tree.
  • additionally for each:
    • ship +4 gold
    • fort +2 gold, +1 tree
    • cementery +4 gold
    • bonfire +5 gold

This change is made to prevent ending the game because there is no more trees on any island. Also each turn two new trees grow up randomly on the island next to the other tree. So they will grown only when there is at last one tree!
